About Me

I'm a Senior-level data analyst with over 10 years of direct experience.

I started my career as data analyst at Prudential Financial, developing large-scale reports, applying a variety of tools and analytical techniques to solve complex problems and drive business decisions. I have experience providing technical expertise and leading projects to automate business processes meeting changing business demands and communicating insights to non-technical audiences.

I earned my Masters in Information Systems from New Jersey Institute of Technology, Newark. In 2016, I created @WitSanDiego, a local group for professional women in technology careers. When I'm not tackling new tech projects, I'm learning to play guitar, dancing salsa or learning about real estate investment.
